Cute Pink Bread

This pink bread features a soft, fluffy texture and a gentle pastel color that gives it a warm and inviting appearance.

Lightly sweet with an airy crumb, each slice feels delicate and comforting, making it well suited for relaxed mornings, tea breaks, or simple, cozy moments.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup beetroot puree
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 egg

Toppings (optional)

  • Powdered sugar
  • Pink Sprinkles
  • Strawberry Glaze

Instructions

  1. Activate Yeast: Warm milk, sugar & yeast. Let sit 5 minutes.
  2. Mix the Dough: Add egg, butter, beet puree & salt.
  3. Knead the Dough: Add flour & knead 8-10 minutes.
  4. First Rise: Let rise 1 hour until doubled.
  5. Shape & Rise: Shape loaf, rest 30 minutes.
  6. Bake: 350Â°F for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Cool & Decorate: Dust or glaze!
