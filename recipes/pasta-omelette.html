Pasta Omelette

A simple pasta omelette made with tender noodles and lightly cooked eggs, offering a soft texture and a comforting, familiar flavor.

Each bite combines the gentle chew of pasta with the fluffiness of eggs, creating a warm, cozy dish that's perfect for a quick meal, a light lunch, or a comforting snack at any time of day.

Ingredients

  • Leftover cooked pasta
  • 2 eggs
  • 1-2 tablespoons flour
  • Pinch of baking powder
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the cooked pasta and mix to coat.
  3. Heat a pan with oil. Pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Cook until golden on both sides.
